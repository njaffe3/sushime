about

Inspired by her mother’s meticulous bento box lunches, Chef Kiko Yamazaki opened Sushi Me in Highland Park in 2019. With a focus on seasonal produce and fresh caught fare, the menu features a rotating cast of favorites as well as specialty rolls. Catch them while you can!
